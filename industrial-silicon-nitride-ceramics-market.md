<p><p>The Industrial Silicon Nitride Ceramics market is segmented into three main types: Reaction Sintered (RS), Gas Pressure Sintered (GPS), and Cold Pressed Sintered (CPS) ceramics. RS ceramics are produced through a chemical reaction that forms silicon nitride during high-temperature processing, resulting in high strength and toughness. GPS ceramics involve sintering under controlled gas pressure, enhancing density and thermal stability. CPS ceramics are made by compacting silicon nitride powder under cold pressure, yielding lightweight, versatile materials suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
