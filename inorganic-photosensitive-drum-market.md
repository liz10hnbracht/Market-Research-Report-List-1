<p><p>The inorganic photosensitive drum market comprises various types, including selenium, selenium alloy, cadmium sulfide, and amorphous silicon. Selenium drums offer good sensitivity and resolution, making them popular in copiers and printers. Selenium alloy enhances performance by improving durability and light sensitivity. Cadmium sulfide provides high sensitivity to light but poses environmental concerns. Amorphous silicon is favored for its versatility and cost-effectiveness, offering stable performance across various applications. Each type serves specific needs in the imaging and printing industry.</p></p>
