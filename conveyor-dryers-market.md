<p><p>The conveyor dryers market includes various types, primarily featuring conveyor belt dryers and infrared conveyor dryers. Conveyor belt dryers utilize a continuous loop of belt to transport materials through a heated drying chamber, effectively removing moisture. In contrast, infrared conveyor dryers employ infrared radiation to rapidly heat and dry materials, ensuring uniform treatment and shorter drying times. Both types are widely used in industries like food processing, textiles, and pharmaceuticals, enhancing efficiency and product quality through effective moisture control.</p></p>
