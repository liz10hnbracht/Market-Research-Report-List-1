<p><p>The cryosorption vacuum pump market comprises various pump types, each with distinct mechanisms. Rotary vane vacuum pumps utilize rotating blades for gas compression, while diaphragm vacuum pumps operate through flexible membranes to create a vacuum. Liquid ring vacuum pumps leverage a ring of liquid to trap gases, ensuring continuous operation. Scroll vacuum pumps use two spirals to compress gas, offering low vibration and noise. Turbomolecular vacuum pumps are high-speed turbines that efficiently evacuate gases at ultra-high vacuum levels, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
