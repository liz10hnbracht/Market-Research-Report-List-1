<p><p>Railway axle counting systems are crucial for tracking train movements and ensuring safety. </p><p>Rail side installation involves sensors placed alongside the tracks to detect passing axles as trains move by, providing accurate real-time data without modifying the rail infrastructure. </p><p>On-rail installation, in contrast, integrates sensors directly onto the rail or train itself, allowing for enhanced precision and the ability to gather data from multiple angles. Both types serve to improve operational efficiency and train monitoring in rail networks.</p></p>
